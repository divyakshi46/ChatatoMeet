Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create a new meeting?

To create a new meeting, navigate to the "Create Meeting" section and follow the instructions. You can invite participants by sharing the meeting link or code.

2. How can I join an existing meeting?

You can join an existing meeting by clicking on the "Join Meeting" button and entering the meeting code provided by the host.

3. What should I do if I experience connection issues?

If you experience connection issues, please check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, contact our support team.
